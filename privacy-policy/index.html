Privacy Policy for Christian Social Application

Last Updated: 28.08.2023

This Privacy Policy describes how the Christian social application collects, uses, discloses, and processes users' personal information. We aim to ensure the protection of your personal information and uphold your privacy.

Collected Information

  1. Account and Profile: Upon registration in the application, we may collect basic information such as your name, email address, and profile picture. This information is used to create and manage your account.

  2. Social Information: Your activity within the application, including posts, comments, and interactions with other users, may be saved and displayed on your profile.

  3. Contact Information: If you choose to connect your contacts from your address book, we may access your contacts to facilitate friend discovery and addition.

  4. Technical Information: We may automatically collect technical information, such as IP address, device type, operating system, and browser information, to enhance app functionality and security.

  5. Hashed Passwords: During registration and authentication, your password is hashed using the SHA-512 algorithm to ensure account security.

Use of Information

  1. We use your information to provide and enhance application functionality, including personalized content, communication with other users, and security.

  2. Your contact information may be used to provide notifications about friends, events, and updates within the application.

  3. Technical information may be used for analyzing app usage, resolving technical issues, and ensuring security.

Disclosure of Information

  1. We do not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

Security

  1. We take measures to protect your personal information, including password hashing and application-level security measures.

Consent

  1. By using the application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Changes to Policy

  1. We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted within the application, and your continued use of the application will signify your agreement with the updated policy.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at i128saagaye.v@inbox.ru.
